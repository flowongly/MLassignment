Predictions Features

Harnessing the power of data, our innovative prediction app analyzes age, systolic and diastolic blood pressure, blood sugar levels, body temperature, and heart rate to provide personalized insights into your maternal health risks. With early knowledge and proactive preparation, you can confidently navigate your pregnancy journey, ensuring the best possible outcomes for you and your baby.

  • Blood Sugar

    High blood sugar levels, such as those seen in gestational diabetes, can increase the risk of complications like preeclampsia, preterm birth, and macrosomia (large birth weight).

  • Age

    Age plays a significant role in maternal health, influencing factors such as fertility, pregnancy risks, and overall well-being.

  • Heart Rate

    Heart rate is an important indicator of cardiovascular health and overall well-being during pregnancy.

  • Body Temperature

    Body temperature fluctuations can provide insights into various aspects of maternal health, such as potential infections or hormonal imbalances.

  • Systolic Blood Pressure

    Maintaining a healthy systolic blood pressure level is crucial for maternal well-being as high blood pressure can increase the risk of complications during pregnancy.

  • Diastolic Blood Pressure

    Diastolic blood pressure affects maternal health by indicating the pressure in your blood vessels between heartbeats.
